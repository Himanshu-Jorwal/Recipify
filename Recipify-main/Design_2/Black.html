1

Add the ingredients you have at home.

You can choose an ingredient from the list or from your saved inventory.

Remember: if an ingredient is not available in the default list, simply type its name in the search bar and add it.

2

Choose what food you want to cook.

You can choose Breakfast, Lunch, Snack, or Dinner.

PantryChef will then recommend recipes that are suitable for the meal you selected.

3

Choose the kitchen equipment you have.

Select kitchen equipment that you have or want to use.

This will prevent PantryChef from recommending recipes using equipment that you don't have or don't want to use.

4

Choose how much time you have.

Choose 5 minutes if you’re in a hurry or longer if you have time.

This will prevent PantryChef from recommending recipes that take too long to prepare.

⏰ 5 minutes
5

Choose how much experience you have with cooking.

You can choose Rookie, Novice or Expert.

Recipify will recommend recipes that suit your skill level.
